02 / Research & publications

Curious about how organisations actually transform.

My research sits where strategy, digital transformation and the cultural and creative industries meet.

Master's research · Global MBA

Factors Influencing Digital Transformation Maturity and Its Impact on Business Performance in Tunisian Cultural and Creative Industries

A mixed-methods study of 96 Tunisian CCI SMEs and five company representatives. It finds that managerial orientation, operational practices and leadership-led experimentation shape digital maturity, while higher maturity is associated with stronger efficiency, audience reach and creative output.

EKA University of Applied Sciences 96 SMEs + 5 interviews Completed 2026

Digital Transformation and Its Impact on Business Models in the Cultural and Creative Industries: Evidence from Latvia

Co-authored with Kaspars Steinbergs and Renāte Cāne. Finds that digitalisation expands market reach, while deeper change in internal processes remains constrained by fragmented strategy, finance and skills.
